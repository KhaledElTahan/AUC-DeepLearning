Problem Statement

Life threatening situations such as armed robberies and lone wolf attacks could take place any time which leaves innocent people defenseless and without any proper arrangement to face the situation, what we are proposing is a system that detects active shooters carrying any type of arms and gives their number and coordinates.

Dataset

We have two datasets: First Type: is from Kaggle and contains 333 images that represents armed men (one or more per image) on the scene of action. Second Type: one contains 1200 images of different type of guns (one per image)

Results

After concatenating the two datasets and having as train and validation 200 images from the first type and 1000 from the second type, the model detected 5% from the test dataset of the first type and 90% from the test dataset of the second type.

Technical report

  • YOLO implmenetation using keras and tensorflow
  • The training was done using google colab
  • Four hours
  • maximum number of epochs reached was 200
  • about 1.2 min/epoch

Conclusion

YOLO model needed more dataset with more diversity to get better performance on general cases.
